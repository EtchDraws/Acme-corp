About Acme Corporation

Acme Corporation provides secure infrastructure and managed services to enterprise clients around the world. Founded in 2003, Acme operates data centers and offers consulting, managed cloud, and platform engineering services.

HQ: San Francisco, CA | Offices: Berlin, Tokyo, Salt Lake City

Client Focus

We serve finance, healthcare, and logistics clients that require resilient systems and strict compliance.

Approach

We combine engineering, automation, and continuous monitoring to reduce downtime and operational risk.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to enable dependable technology at scale. We focus on:

Reliability

Designing systems that keep services online under pressure.

Security

Implementing defenses and processes that reduce exposure and risk.

Partnership

Working closely with clients to deliver tailored, measurable results.

Services

  • Managed Cloud - end-to-end cloud migration and operations
  • Server Operations - dedicated server management and monitoring
  • Security & Compliance - audits, testing, and continuous monitoring
  • Professional Services - architecture, automation, and runbooks
